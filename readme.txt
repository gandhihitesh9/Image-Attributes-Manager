Automatically generate and manage image "ALT" attributes in bulk or individually, enhancing SEO and accessibility with ease.

== Description ==

In the era of AI-driven tools, optimizing your website for both users and search engines is more important than ever. However, incorporating artificial intelligence is often costly and complex. That’s why we developed the Image Attributes Manager plugin—a user-friendly solution designed to automate and streamline the management of image "ALT" attributes.

With Image Attributes Manager, you can quickly generate ALT tags for your images in bulk or individually, saving valuable time and ensuring consistent image metadata throughout your site. The plugin seamlessly adds an "ALT" column to the WordPress media library’s list view, making it easy to review and update your images’ ALT text at a glance. Perfect for improving accessibility and boosting your SEO, this tool is a must-have for WordPress site owners looking to leverage automation for smarter content management.


== Installation ==

The plugin is simple to install:

 * Download image-attributes-manager.zip
 * Unzip
 * Upload image-attributes-manager directory to your /wp-content/plugins directory
 * Activate the "Image Attributes Manager" list plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
